Soar

Challenges

I started developing the mobile game last winter with no experience in android or even Git, and it was my first full project. Before Soar, I'd only coded for school assignments and small projects but I came to learn that making a project on your own involves a completely different process. There was no teacher to guide me when I got confused, so I had to find my own solutions. I got into the pattern of googling and using youtube videos effectively to make my game, and I learned to enjoy it.

The other big challenge was organizing the assets and correctly designing the animations. Perfecting sprites and background images took hours longer than I predicted. When I started developing, I hadn't considered all the resources I could have used to save time, which is what led to this mistake.

Lessons

Time management, organization, and code clarity are key. Hours spent debugging weird code could have been spent breaking down the tasks into more manageable pieces first. Since I didn't have any actual experience outside of easy little coding projects, I thought I could develop the whole thing without much planning (big mistake). As a first project, I'm satisfied with what I managed to complete, but I'll always cringe a little when I think back to how I took on this project.
